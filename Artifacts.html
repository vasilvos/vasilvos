What are Artifacts?

Artifacts are items in Genshin Impact that can be equipped on Characters to increase their Stats. They play an important for your character especially your main DPS. 5 star artifacts can be upgraded to level 20 max.

In other words, with better artifact stats and upgraded, the more stronger your main DPS will become. Here I will showcase my main DPS's artifacts to show you an example of their stats.
